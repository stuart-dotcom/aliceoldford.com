About Alice

Writer, Realtor,
Florida Native at Heart

Alice Oldford wears many hats — author, adventurer, herbalist, wife, mother, grandmother, and licensed Associate Real Estate Broker. She has called Lake Placid, Florida home for a decade and has grown very familiar with the landscapes, waterways, and communities.

Her books bring Florida’s outdoor treasures to life for hikers, paddlers, and curious explorers. Her real estate practice, built on more than 35 years of experience, is guided by a simple belief: “I don’t sell homes. I help people realize their real estate dreams.”
