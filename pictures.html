Local Community & Organisations

Places and organisations Alice is proud to support in the Lake Placid area.

  • Lake Placid Mural Society

    Celebrating Lake Placid’s world-famous outdoor mural collection — Referred to as the Town of Murals with 50 hand-painted murals. Nicknamed "The Caladium Capital of the World", due to the caladium development initiated by Dr. Robert Hartman

  • Lake Placid Garden Club

    The Lake Placid Garden Club has beautified and enriched the community for generations — planting public gardens, funding civic improvements, and fostering a love of horticulture among residents of all ages. Alice is a proud member and supporter of the club’s ongoing mission to keep Lake Placid blooming.

  • Change of Pace Caregivers Support Group

    Change of Pace offers vital support groups for caregivers in the Lake Placid area — providing community, resources, and compassionate guidance for those who dedicate themselves to caring for others. Alice is proud member and supports this important organisation.

  • Lake Placid Chamber of Commerce

    The Lake Placid Chamber of Commerce champions local business, community events, and economic vitality in Highlands County. Alice volunteers with the Chamber and is committed to helping Lake Placid thrive as a destination for visitors and residents alike.

Nature & Outdoor Resources

Alice’s recommended resources for outdoor explorers and nature lovers.

  • Highlands County Audubon Society

    Wildlife conservation and birding in Highlands County, Florida.

  • Archbold Biological Station

    World-class ecological research station in Venus, Florida.

  • Six Mile Cypress Slough Preserve

    A two-mile boardwalk through one of Southwest Florida’s finest wetland ecosystems.

  • Miss Potter’s Place

    A beloved herbalist destination in Fort Myers, Florida — Miss Potter’s Place is a haven for anyone passionate about medicinal plants, herbal remedies, and living in harmony with the natural world. A kindred spirit to Alice’s own love of nature and holistic living.

  • Pineland Farms

    New Gloucester, Maine — agricultural heritage and outdoor education.

  • The Unruly Gardener

    Honest, joyful writing about gardening in all its wild glory.
