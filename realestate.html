Alice Oldford is a transplant to beautiful Lake Placid, Florida — and she wouldn't have it any other way. With more than 25 years of experience in real estate transactions, she brings a natural and professional attention to detail that makes the process smooth, whether you are a first-time buyer, an empty nester, a growing family, or an aspiring snowbird.

Before becoming a Realtor, Alice worked as a legal secretary specialising in real estate transactions, served as a land use board administrator, and was a sales manager for builders marketing new communities — handling everything from explaining blueprints to closing title. That deep background means she understands every step of a transaction from every angle.

Alice is a Florida Real Estate Broker Associate and a Graduate of the Realtor's Institute. She is a member of the Heartland Association of Realtors and a 16-year veteran of the American Cancer Society Relay for Life. She also volunteers with the Mural Society of Lake Placid, the Lake Placid Garden Club, and the Women's Club.
