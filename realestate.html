Why Lake Placid?

Nestled in the heart of Highlands County, Lake Placid is one of Florida's best-kept secrets — a small town surrounded by lakes, trails, and wide-open natural beauty. Alice has lived and worked here for decades and knows every neighbourhood, every waterway, and every opportunity the area has to offer. Discover everything the area has to offer at the official Highlands County visitor guide.

For nature lovers, the area is home to the Highlands County Audubon Society — a testament to the extraordinary wildlife and natural environment that makes this region so special.
