Florida on Land and Water

10 Adventures and More in Lee & Collier Counties

Where can you enjoy an active outdoor experience in bustling Lee and Collier Counties? Alice answers that question with first-hand trail guides, paddling routes, and hidden natural gems across Southwest Florida — written for adventurers of every fitness level.

Featuring trails at Archbold Biological Station and paddle routes through the Six Mile Cypress Slough Preserve, this guide puts Southwest Florida's finest natural areas within reach.

Alice received national recognition for this work — read the Sebring Highland Sun article here.
