Books by Alice Oldford

Outdoor adventure guides for Florida and New Jersey, and a cookbook that turns everyday meals into something worth remembering.

Florida · Outdoor Adventure · Published 2017

10 Adventures and More in Lee & Collier Counties: Florida on Land and Water

President's Gold Medal — Florida Authors and Publishers Association

Where can you enjoy an active outdoor experience in bustling Lee and Collier counties? Interested in local history? Alice answers both questions with first-hand trail guides, paddling routes, and hidden natural gems across Southwest Florida.

Discover how the Monday Group — a bunch of 1970s teenagers — influenced the development of Six Mile Cypress Slough Preserve and Manatee Park. Learn about the utopian Koreshan community. Visit Everglades Wonder Gardens, once a beloved roadside attraction. Experience rare Florida scrub at Hickey's Creek Mitigation Park. And explore water routes perfect for a leisurely paddle.

Adventure right here in your own backyard.

Cookbook · Life & Food

Recipes and Life: Life is Too Short to be Stuck in the Kitchen

"One way to get the most out of life is to look upon it as an adventure." — William Feather

Eating — a habit, a pleasure — guided by the seasons of the year and of our lives. Do you want to liven up your family's meals, maximize your food budget, and limit your time in the kitchen?

This is Alice's collection of inspirations sprinkled with favourite recipes: practical tips from a chef and food-loving friends, written for every level of cook — whether you live alone or feed a crowd, whether you eat meat or not, whether you're dieting or indulging.

New Jersey · Hiking Guide · Co-authored with Sue Dziamara

You Can Get There From Here: Hiking Hunterdon County Trails

Hunterdon, Warren and Morris counties in New Jersey are part of the Skylands Region — bounded by the Delaware River with views into Pennsylvania. Farm fields, forests, hills, wildlife, and four reservoirs sit alongside historic homes, farms, and estates.

Alice's first book, co-written with Sue Dziamara, grew from years of walking and exploring Hunterdon County and wanting to share the wealth. Goslings and tadpoles in spring; glistening virgin snow in winter — enjoy the trails year round.

New Jersey · Hiking Guide · Sequel

Hiking NJ Trails — Hunterdon County and Beyond: You Can Get There From Here Too

The sequel to You Can Get There From Here broadens the quest — extending into the Highlands trails and beyond Hunterdon County into the wider Skylands Region. More paths, more history, more hidden treasures to discover.

Both New Jersey hiking guides are available together. Bring your camera: there is always something worth photographing, every season of the year.
